Welcome to Our Church

Welcome to MFM CAASO Youth Church, Upperroom Tabernacle. We are a vibrant community committed to prayer, the Word of God, and raising a generation of youths who are passionate about God's kingdom. Our mission is to equip young people with spiritual tools to impact their world for Christ.
